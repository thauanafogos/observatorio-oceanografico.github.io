Hello, I am Giuliana!

I am a Cultural Production student at the Fluminense Federal University (UFF), and throughout my academic journey I have been seeking to integrate cultural management, digital communication, and content production. I believe digital platforms are essential tools for expanding access to information, highlighting scientific initiatives, and bringing different audiences closer to topics that matter to society.

Recently, I joined the Oceanographic Observatory (O2) team, where I work in communication and scientific dissemination. My goal is to help turn marine science topics into accessible, creative, and engaging content, strengthening the dialogue between university, science, and community.

At O2, I aim to contribute to the development of digital strategies that increase the visibility of the group's work and bring the public closer to its research, educational actions, and ocean literacy initiatives.

When I am not at university or involved in O2 projects, I am probably reading, listening to music, or studying Italian - activities that help keep my mind creative and open to new forms of expression.
