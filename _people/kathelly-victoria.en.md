Hello! My name is Kathelly Victoria.

I am a Cinema and Audiovisual student, working in cultural production, communication, and audiovisual media. I have been building a path focused on the creation, mediation, and dissemination of cultural content, especially in projects that connect art, technology, and communication.

Currently, I am part of Liga de Animacao Acetato and I work as a cultural dissemination support fellow at the Oceanographic Observatory (O2), contributing to content production and science communication. I also participate in the Cinema and Audiovisual Observatory (OCA), where I work as a review editor.

My academic experience includes serving as a teaching assistant in animation, in addition to participating as a curator and mediator at the UFFilme showcase. I also interned at the National Film Agency (Ancine), an experience that broadened my understanding of the audiovisual sector and its institutional, cultural, and regulatory dimensions.

Beyond audiovisual work, I served as a marketing advisor for the MinervaBots robotics team, developing activities related to communication, outreach, and content production.

I am interested in expanding my experience in creative, cultural, and audiovisual projects, especially initiatives that bring together communication, science, art, and technology.
