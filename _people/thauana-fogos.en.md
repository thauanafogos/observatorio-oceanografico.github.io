## Hi! I'm Thauana. ✨🦋

I'm an undergraduate student in Environmental Science at Fluminense Federal University (UFF) and currently serve as an **Extension Scholarship Holder** in the **Oceanographic Observatory – Ocean Literacy in Action** project.

Throughout my undergraduate journey, I have been developing skills in geotechnologies, spatial analysis, and environmental data interpretation, seeking to integrate science, education, and conservation across different contexts. I am particularly interested in biodiversity conservation, climate change, and environmental management, and I strongly believe that bringing scientific knowledge closer to society is essential for building a more sustainable future.

Passionate about the ocean 🌊💙, I strive to understand and strengthen the connections between people and coastal environments, contributing to a greater awareness of the importance of the oceans for life on our planet.

When I'm not reading countless articles, you'll probably find me spending time with my family and my three dogs 🐶, or enjoying some of my favorite hobbies, such as reading, listening to music, and watching the silly things I love.

📫 I'm always open to new experiences, collaborations, and opportunities to exchange knowledge.
